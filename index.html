ABOUT ALUSB

The African Leadership University School of Business (ALUSB) is part of the African Leadership Group’s (ALG) portfolio of companies and is accredited by the Higher Education Council of Rwanda.

The school offers a portfolio of global business programs with a strong focus on the African Continental Free Trade Area (AfCFTA) and African & Global Case Studies. You learn by doing and solving problems related to your community.

Your desired Global Pan-African Business School delivered to you!!!





Executive MBA

Features—The two-year degree offers:

  • A curriculum with a range of electives at the forefront of business trends offered in a hybrid format
  • Faculty with both theoretical grounding and business experience
  • Course materials and discussion contextualized to the challenges of Africa/AFCTA
  • Hub locations in several major cities across the Continent
  • Project-focused problem-solving in major African capital during Intensives

Fast Forward

Our non-degree certification programs may be the answer, offering:

  • Annual subscription to a menu of self-contained online courses, workshops and immersive programs through which to earn certification
  • Self- directed and self-paced progression to align with your needs and interests
  • Course materials and discussion contextualized to the challenges of Africa/AFCTA
  • Lifelong learning that can include one course or one hundred from graduation to retirement
  • Flexibility in converting accumulated certificates to degree programs, under conditions
